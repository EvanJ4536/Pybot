These scripts are sometimes hard to stop because they move your mouse and use your keyboard.
A simple CTRL + C while Powershell or CMD is the active window will stop it.

Run this script while in the Lumbridge cow pen, other places with lots of cows may work too.
It will attempt to detect false positives and prevent clicking on the same spot over and over.
Because of this it may get stuck in certain places for a while until its certain it has a match.

it will fight cows until you stop it.
It will NOT stop or run away if youre almost dead.
